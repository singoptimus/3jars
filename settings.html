Welcome to 3 Jars! 🍯

3 Jars teaches kids that every dollar they earn has three jobs: experiencing life with family, investing for the future, and giving back to the world. Every time your child earns 10,000 points through games and practice, their three jars fill up with real money that you configure below.

The idea is simple but powerful: instead of just handing out allowances, kids earn their rewards through learning. They see their jars fill up, learn to split money into experiences, savings, and generosity — and start building financial habits that last a lifetime.

We recommend starting with $50 for experiences, $10 for investing, and $10 for giving back. You can always adjust these amounts later. Set up your jars below, then start playing!
