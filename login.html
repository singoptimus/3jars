🍯 3 JARS ACADEMY

Every Game Fills a Jar.

I built 3Jars for my kids because I wanted Math and Hebrew practice to lead to the things that actually matter. Instead of just "get gifts" — kids can earn experiences.

Kids play toward a goal of 10,000 points. When they hit it, they unlock their 3 Jars:

  • 🎉 The Family Jar — We skip the "stuff" and choose a memory: movie nights, the arcade, or ice cream (whatever your family likes).
  • 💰 The Investing Jar — They get their first taste of investing and learn how to track it with the parents.
  • 💝 The Giving Jar — They experience the joy of choosing a cause and giving back to the community.

Every family is different, so you decide the reward. In our home, 10,000 points unlocks $50 for a family outing, $10 for their portfolio, and $10 for charity.
